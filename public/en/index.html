Mentoring

Mentoring with me

I have created unique methodology - BMV ®,which combines the practices of coaching (ICF), vocal yoga and traditional EVT (EVT is a vocal science that explains functions in vocals and anatomy)

This is a path to success, self-expression, and disclosure of your potential through your BODY, MIND and VOICE.

Why mentoring?

It will allow you to work out of your clamps and blocks both psychologically and bodily, show your professional,true life potential, return to natural freedom, sexuality, self-confidence

Way to success

I won the grand award at the international competition at the cost of my voice!

I lost my voice for 1 year, because of illness and neglect of teachers, who didn't listen to me saying, that I couldn't sing. I undergone through a therapy, psychologist for 6 months and psychosamothology with vocal yoga. Only 2 last gave to me the result of my voice return.

I released/liberated myself from the fears of sounding socially unacceptable, began to manifest myself, to be more confident. At the age of 19, I have begun to study psychosomatology & voice anatomy. The effort rewarded me with certification, what encouraged me to go also on a coaching course.

Frequently asked questions

  • Will I get specific tasks to work on my voice?

    Of course, each video includes a text description of tasks for independent work, as well as audio exersises.

  • Will I learn something new in these courses?

    All courses are the result of experience, practices of being a vocalist and teacher. All the information that I have, I did not get somewhere from "space," this is a long-term work, learnig new informations on American EVT and CVT training, vocal rehabilitation, exchange of experience with other vocal teachers, reading foreign textbooks and YouTube - I checked all my knowledge on myself, my students and choosed what works best.  

    I do not guarantee that all information for you in these courses will be exclusive, or any special. It all depends on your experience and your goals.

    If you are a teacher, then perhaps you will be interested in how I "teach" information, which is my teaching method.

    If you are a vocalist "in search," then of course you will learn a lot of useful and new information. It is also important to understand that the information in the courses already "filtered" and "grouped" for convenient and effective learning.

  • How quickly will I learn what is shown in the courses?

    It all depends on your level and regularity of classes. If you are new to voice production, it takes from 6 month to a year. If you are a vocalist with experience - 2-3 months to master this or other vocal "concept." Provided that you practice 2-3 times a week regularly.

  • Will I need any special equipment for classes in these workshops?

    No, for these courses you only need a phone or laptop, stable internet and a good mood

  • Is it possible to get feedback from the teacher if something is not clear or there are difficulties with performing tasks?

    Feedback on short questions will be possible if you order a consultation that lasts 30 minutes. It is free once for a person. In addition to it, you can also order private lessons. More you can ask on instagram @iamsavbea
